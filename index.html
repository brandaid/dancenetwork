We love watching dancing on TV, viewing the best videos online, going to competitions, and we love to dance ourselves. But it’s never been all in one place. It will be soon.

It’s more than just watching. It’s about connecting through Dance. Dance Network will provide a dynamic range of resources for Dance lovers to engage the world of Dance every way possible.

If you love Dance as much as we do, we want your advice in creating Dance Network. We’re looking for those passionate few who will help us make content choices, give feedback on original shows, and be the first to see the new network.
